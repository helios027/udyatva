ABOUT US

“A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step”

Udyatva, whose name is derived from the SANSKRIT word 'UDYAT' meaning Hard Work, Discipline, Trained or Exercised,is the first edition of our intra-sports meet.












Whether you’re a regular participant, or looking to join your first ever Sports Tournament, you’ve come to the right place. UDYATVA 2020 is an engaging and fun event.

We believe that fitness equals happiness, and encourage our participants to challenge their bodies while maintaining a healthy lifestyle. For more information, take a look at our site. With the gleaming floodlights shimmering off the grass and the tranquil waters, let's get this started.

Q-As
When and where is it happening?
The 2020 edition of UDYATVA will take place from 08th Feb 2020 to 10th Feb 2020 at the IIIT Bhagalpur campus.
Can one person participate in more than one event?
Yes. In case that the events clash the responsibility will be of the participant and not of the organisers.
Who can participate?
Registration is open to all students across the years and departments in a variety of sporting events. Please refer to the corresponding rulebooks for eligibility details.
When and how will the prizes be distributed?
All prizes of the event, whatsoever nature, will be given only after Udyatva 2020 is over . All decisions of the organizing committee with regard to any event will be final and binding on all.
Will on the spot registrations be entertained?
On the spot registrations will be entertained only when a sporting event/events has/ have not met its/their team cap. We would advise you to register online since participation is on a first-come-first-serve basis.
How do I register for the event?
Teams and individuals must register themselves. The closing date for registration varies from event to event and depends on the availability of slots. The same can be seen in . Once paid, the registration fee is not refundable.
